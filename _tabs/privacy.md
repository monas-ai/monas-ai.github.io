<h2>Privacy</h2>
<p>This site is designed to be readable without tracking. We only collect minimal analytics when enabled.</p>

<h3>Analytics</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Provider: Umami (privacy-friendly, cookie-free).</li>
  <li>Events: page views, engagement time, scroll depth, outbound clicks, search usage, share actions, and subscribe submits.</li>
  <li>We respect <code>Do Not Track</code> (DNT). If your browser sends DNT=1, analytics is disabled.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Contact</h3>
<p>If you have questions about data handling, contact the site owner via the About page.</p>
